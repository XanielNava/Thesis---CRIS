About CRIS

Community-Centric Rabies Intelligence System (CRIS)

The Community-Centric Rabies Intelligence System (CRIS) is a web-based and mobile-supported platform designed to improve rabies surveillance, reporting, and management in Iloilo Province.

Rabies remains a serious public health concern in the Philippines, and existing systems are often fragmented and manual. CRIS provides a centralized, data-driven solution that connects communities, health authorities, and veterinary services.
